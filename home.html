WELCOME TO ECOGUARD BIOSCIENCES

Ecoguard Biosciences (Pty) Ltd is a scientific research company specialised in the development and distribution of products which are effective and yet compatible with the environment, and which carry low risk potential with regards to humans and non-target organisms.

WHAT WE DO

Our products range currently developed is exclusive and specialised, offering selective solutions to a range of vegetation management. Ecoguard offers a network of technically qualified field staff to effectively service the correct and efficient use of these products.

WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT

We provide environmentally compatible solutions for:

INVASIVE PLANT CONTROL

We provide solutions for invasive plant species management. Invasive species use a large amount of water, can increase fuel loads and contributes to erosion.

FORESTRY WEED CONTROL

We provide weed control solutions to reduce competition between newly planted trees and other vegetation as well as to manage the vegetation within plantations for safety reasons.

SELECTIVE BUSH CONTROL

We provide solutions for a soft approach to bush encroachment and has a unique history around the implementation of selective bush control.

TURFGRASS AND INDUSTRIAL WEED CONTROL

We provide solutions for weed control in parks, golf courses, lawns as well as industrial areas such as railways roads, pavements, electrical substation and fence lines.

AQUATIC WEED CONTROL

We provide solutions to manage invasive alien plants and other vegetation that infest our rivers and dams.

ARID REGION VEGETATION MANAGEMENT

We provide solutions for Prosopis species and to a variety of cactus species in the arid and semi-arid regions of South Africa

ADJUVANTS AND DYES

We can provide you with the adjuvants that are added to a herbicide mixture to improve its performance in several ways. Dyes are added to the herbicide mixture so that treated plants/stumps could be marked to avoid re-application.

EQUIPMENT

We have all of the equipment you may need for the application of our products.
